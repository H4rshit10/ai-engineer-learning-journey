# AI Strategist

## Who is an AI Strategist?
An AI Strategist is a professional who designs and guides
the adoption of AI solutions to align with business goals.

They focus on planning, decision-making, and impact
rather than only model development.

---

## Key Responsibilities of an AI Strategist
- Identify business problems that can be solved using AI
- Decide when to use AI, RAG, or fine-tuning
- Select appropriate models, tools, and platforms
- Balance performance, cost, and scalability
- Ensure ethical and responsible AI usage
- Measure ROI and business impact of AI systems

---

## Skills Required
- Understanding of AI and ML concepts
- Knowledge of LLMs, RAG, and fine-tuning
- Cost and API usage awareness
- Data understanding
- Communication and decision-making skills
- Strategic thinking

---

## AI Strategist vs AI Engineer
- AI Engineer focuses on implementation and coding
- AI Strategist focuses on planning, architecture, and impact

Both roles often work together.

---

## Importance of AI Strategy
- Prevents unnecessary AI costs
- Ensures AI aligns with business needs
- Improves scalability and sustainability
- Reduces risk and failure of AI projects
