# AI Engineer

## Who is an AI Engineer?
An AI Engineer is responsible for designing, building,
deploying, and maintaining AI-powered systems and applications.

They work at the intersection of software engineering,
machine learning, and data systems.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Build and integrate AI models into applications
- Develop RAG-based and LLM-powered systems
- Work with APIs, vector databases, and embeddings
- Optimize performance, cost, and scalability
- Monitor and evaluate AI systems in production
- Collaborate with data scientists and product teams

---

## Skills Required
- Python programming
- Understanding of AI and ML concepts
- Knowledge of LLMs and transformers
- Experience with RAG and vector databases
- API integration
- Evaluation and monitoring

---

## Tools Commonly Used
- Python
- Hugging Face
- LangChain
- Vector databases (FAISS, Pinecone, etc.)
- Cloud platforms
- AI APIs

---

## AI Engineer vs Other Roles
- AI Engineer focuses on building and deployment
- Data Scientist focuses on analysis and experimentation
- AI Strategist focuses on planning and decision-making
